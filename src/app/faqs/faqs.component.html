Every year, Darshan University-Rajkot used to organise a State Level Technical Symposium under the name "Frolic". It is organised every year in the month of September. Frolic is a series of technical and non-technical competitions and events covering all areas of engineering, science, and management. The attraction of this event is that it covers many technical and entrepreneurship events, where students from various institutes come up with their innovative ideas, take part, win and are motivated by rewards.

There are no registration charges for any technical event.

All participants who successfully take part in any technical events and satisfy eligibility criteria (if any) are eligible for a participation certificate. Note that the winner will get a rank certificate in a hard copy and not a participation certificate. Also refer individual event rules.

No overnight accommodation facilities will be provided to any participant. The participants coming from outstation can stay anywhere in Rajkot, though they can avail bus facilities from Rajkot city to the university campus as per the mentioned schedule and bus routes only. Refer general rules also.

No free food facility is available, but participants can avail their lunch/breakfast from the canteen on a chargeable basis.

The participants from other institutes can also avail the bus facility provided by Darshan University on the day of an event only from the specific city mentioned in the confirmation email. As well, participants can directly come to the university campus with their institute I-card. RMTS bus facilities are also available. Check for RMTS bus routes.
